SIZE CHARTS
 

The biggest challenge for any online store to overcome is the absence of physical contact between the customer and the products. We have to pull-out every trick in the book, features like: Product Zoom, shots from multiple angles, on and off model, invisible mannequins, informative descriptions and reviews from customers who own the product; to help get the prospective buyer as close to a tactile interaction with the product as possible. Size Charts are an extremely important part of this…

ORGANISING DISPLAY ORDER OF PRODUCT (ONLINE MERCHANDISING)
 

Merchandising product on your website is as important as merchandising product in your B&M Flagship Stores. The principle is exactly the same. If anything, it requires more attention, because there is a larger range of product available for people to find online. The new platform has been designed & built to have landing pages for Category Level 1 and Category Level 2 navigation. This ensures we’re showcasing the breadth of the range available with minimal clicks from the user and close to the top of the page. For this design to work, all products showing in these Category Level 1 and Level 2 landing pages, needs to be ‘organised with purpose’ by the Brand Team…
